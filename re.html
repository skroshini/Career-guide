Real Estate

Working in real estate means helping people buy, sell, rent, or manage properties such as homes, land, offices, and shops. A real estate professional can be an agent, broker, developer, property manager, or investment advisor, and the job usually involves networking, negotiation, market research, and financial planning.

Key Areas of Real Estate

(The main subjects and topics you will learn or work on in this field)

Real Estate Principles & Practices

Basics of property markets, transactions, and ownership.

Property Law & Regulations

Contracts, property rights, taxation, and legal compliance.

Real Estate Finance & Investment

Mortgages, loans, property funding, and investment analysis.

Property Valuation & Appraisals

Methods to calculate the worth of land and buildings.

Urban Planning & Development

City planning, smart cities, zoning, and infrastructure.

Construction & Property Development

Basics of design, construction, and project management.

Marketing & Sales in Real Estate

Advertising, digital marketing, negotiation, and client relations.

Property Management

Maintenance, renting, leasing, and customer service.

Sustainable & Green Real Estate

Eco-friendly housing and energy-efficient buildings.

PropTech & Digital Real Estate

Using technology (AI, VR tours, blockchain, smart homes).
