Design & Media focuses on creating visuals, animations, videos, and interactive content to inform, entertain, or persuade audiences. It blends art, storytelling, and digital tools.

Design

Design focuses on creativity, problem-solving, and making products or visuals both functional and attractive.

Key Areas in Design

  • Graphic Design (logos, branding, posters)
  • UI/UX Design (apps, websites)
  • Product Design (fashion, industrial, furniture)
  • Interior & Architectural Design
