Gaming & Esports

Gaming refers to the creation, design, and playing of video games. It includes game development, animation, graphics, storytelling, programming, and sound design.

Esports (Electronic Sports) means professional competitive gaming where players or teams compete in tournaments for prizes and recognition.

Difference between Science and Technology

Gaming is about creating and designing video games, while Esports is about playing those games competitively at a professional level.

Best for students who

  • Love video games and technology
  • Are creative and enjoy storytelling, design, or coding
  • Can handle competition and teamwork
  • Want to combine passion for gaming with a career

Not suitable for students who

  • Don’t enjoy technology, creativity, or games
  • Prefer jobs with less screen time
  • Find it hard to handle competition or irregular work schedules

Career Path Flowchart


After 10th Choose Stream

Science (for coding, AI, and development focus)

Arts/Commerce (for design, media, and management focus)

After 12th Appear for Entrance Exams like NID DAT, UCEED, NIFT, DU JAT / CUET etc

Undergraduate Courses B.Sc. in Game Development / Animation, BBA/B.A. in Esports & Digital Media etc

Postgraduate / Specialization M.Sc. in Gaming / Animation, M.A. in Esports Management / Media etc

Career Roles Game Developer / Designer / Tester, Professional Gamer / Coach / Streamer, Esports Event Manager / Analyst etc
