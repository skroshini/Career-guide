Sales and Management

Sales and Management is the field focused on promoting, selling, and managing products or services while leading teams, planning business strategies, and ensuring organizational growth. It combines business, marketing, leadership, and customer relations.

Key Subjects

Business Administration

Basics of business operations and corporate structure

Marketing & Sales

Strategies for promoting and selling products

Human Resource Management

Managing people and teams

Financial Management

Planning and managing budgets and investments

Entrepreneurship & Innovation

Creating and managing new ventures

Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

Building and maintaining client trust

International Business

Understanding global trade and management

Skills You Will Learn

  • Leadership & team management
  • Strategic decision-making
  • Communication & negotiation
  • Problem-solving & critical thinking
  • Sales strategies & digital marketing
  • Customer relationship building
  • Time and project management

Courses in Sales & Management

Undergraduate (UG):

  • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)
  • BMS (Bachelor of Management Studies)
  • B.Com (with Sales/Marketing specialization)
  • BA in Business Management

Postgraduate (PG):

  • MBA in Marketing / Sales / HR / Finance
  • PGDM (Post Graduate Diploma in Management)
  • Master’s in International Business (MIB)
  • Executive MBA

Doctoral (PhD):

  • PhD in Management Studies
  • PhD in Business Administration
  • PhD in Marketing / International Business

Career Opportunities

  • Sales Executive / Manager
  • Marketing Manager
  • Business Development Executive
  • HR Manager
  • Retail Manager
  • Brand Manager
  • Customer Relationship Manager
  • Entrepreneur / Startup Founder
  • International Business Consultant

Best for students who

  • Are confident, outgoing, and persuasive
  • Enjoy leadership roles and teamwork
  • Have strong communication skills
  • Like business, marketing, and problem-solving
  • Are ambitious and career-oriented

Not suitable for students who

  • Dislike interaction with people
  • Avoid challenging/competitive environments
  • Prefer jobs with minimal social communication
  • Are uncomfortable with targets and deadlines

Career Path Flowchart


After 10th Choose Commerce / Arts / Science Stream (Commerce preferred for Business)

After 12th Appear for Entrance Exams like CUET, DU JAT, NPAT, SET, IPMAT

Undergraduate Courses BBA / BMS / B.Com / BA in Management

Postgraduate / Specialization MBA / PGDM / MIB (Marketing, Sales, HR, Finance, International Business)

Doctorate (Optional) PhD in Management / Marketing / International Business

Career Roles Sales Manager, Marketing Executive, Brand Manager, HR Manager, Entrepreneur
