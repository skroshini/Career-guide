Travel

The field of Travel is about exploring destinations, promoting tourism, planning itineraries, and providing services for people who love to discover new places. It connects culture, adventure, hospitality, and business opportunities worldwide.

Key Areas of Study

Tourism Studies

Understanding global destinations, tourism trends, and cultural tourism.

Travel Management

Managing travel agencies, airlines, and trip planning.

Hospitality

Learning hotel operations and guest management for travelers.

Itinerary Planning

Designing trips, packages, and tours for clients.

International Travel

Visa, immigration rules, and overseas travel planning.

Travel Business

Running agencies, startups, or corporate travel solutions.

Skills You Will Gain

  • Trip and itinerary planning
  • Customer service and communication
  • Event and travel management
  • Problem-solving during travel
  • Cross-cultural awareness
  • Business and entrepreneurship
  • Networking in tourism industry

    • Career Path Flowchart


    After 10th Choose any stream (Arts, Commerce, or Science). Focus on English, Geography, Business Studies if possible.

    After 12th Complete Higher Secondary (10+2) from any stream

    Entrance Exams NCHM JEE (Hotel Management & Tourism), CUET (for BA Tourism Studies/Travel courses), state/university entrance exams

    Undergraduation BA Travel & Tourism, BBA Tourism Management, B.Sc. Hospitality & Hotel Administration (via NCHM JEE), BA Aviation & Hospitality, Bachelor of Hotel Management (BHM), etc.

    Postgraduation MA/MBA in Tourism Management, MSc in Hospitality & Tourism, Master of Travel Administration, International MBA in Hospitality/Tourism

    Competitive/Global Exams UGC NET (for research/lectureship), GRE/GMAT + TOEFL/IELTS (for abroad studies), state PSC exams (for tourism departments)

    Career Opportunities Travel Consultant, Tour Manager, Airline Staff, Cruise Management, Event Planner, Travel Blogger, Hotel & Resort Manager, Government Tourism Officer

    PhD (Optional, for Research/Academia) PhD in Tourism & Hospitality, PhD in Travel Management, PhD in Aviation & Tourism Studies
