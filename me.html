Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical engineering is one of the broadest and most versatile fields of engineering.
It focuses on designing, analyzing, manufacturing, and maintaining machines, tools, and mechanical systems using principles of mechanics, thermodynamics, and materials science.

Key Areas of Study

(the main subjects and topics that u will typically learn during ur course)

Mechanics

Study of forces, motion, and structural behavior.

Thermodynamics

Heat, energy, and their transformation in systems.

Fluid Mechanics

Behavior of liquids and gases in motion and rest.

Materials Science

Properties and selection of engineering materials.

Manufacturing

Processes like machining, welding, and 3D printing.

CAD & Simulation

Designing and testing parts using engineering software.

Robotics & Automation

Design and control of automated machines and robots.

Skills You Will Gain

  • Problem-solving and analytical thinking
  • Technical design and drawing
  • Hands-on machine handling
  • Project management
  • Knowledge of safety standards

Career Opportunities

  • Aerospace Engineer
  • Energy Systems Engineer
  • HVAC Engineer
  • Marine Engineer
  • Robotics Engineer
  • Research and Development
  • Government sectors (ISRO, DRDO, Railways, PSU)

Future Trends

  • Electric Vehicles (EVs)
  • Renewable Energy Systems
  • Automation & Industry 4.0
  • 3D Printing and Rapid Prototyping
  • Artificial Intelligence in Engineering
