What to do after 10th?

Many students and their parents think that the options after 10th are limited. But that’s not true.

1. Science Stream

If you're interested in subjects like mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, and technology, you can choose this stream.

This opens up further opportunities for:

  • Engineering (B.Tech, B.E.)
  • Medical Science (MBBS, BDS)
  • Biotechnology
  • Pharmacy
  • Pure Sciences (B.Sc.)
  • Specializations in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics
  • Nursing or Paramedical Courses
  • Research & Development Careers

2. Commerce Stream

If you’re inclined towards subjects like accountancy, business studies, economics, and finance, then commerce is a good fit.

  • Chartered Accountancy (CA)
  • Company Secretary (CS)
  • Cost Accountancy (CMA)
  • Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)
  • BBA/BBM (Business Administration)
  • Banking & Finance
  • Management (MBA in future)

3. Humanities/Arts Stream

If you have an interest in subjects like history, psychology, sociology, literature, or languages, you can opt for this stream.

  • Law (5-year integrated LLB)
  • Journalism & Mass Communication
  • Designing (Fashion, Interior, Graphic Design)
  • Performing Arts (Theater, Music, Dance)
  • Hotel Management
  • Psychology, Sociology
  • Teaching
  • Public Administration (Civil Services)

4. Polytechnic Diploma

If you want to pursue a technical career but prefer to join the workforce after a few years, you can take up a Diploma in Engineering or other fields like:

  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Computer Science

After completing a diploma, you can either go for higher studies (B.Tech) or get a job. Diploma students can join B.Tech in the 2nd year (known as lateral entry).

5. Vocational Courses

Vocational Courses are educational programs that focus on providing practical and hands-on training for specific trades or occupations.

These are ideal for students who prefer skill development over traditional academics.

  • Hospitality Management
  • Event Management
  • Retail Management
  • Automobile Engineering
  • Beauty & Cosmetology
  • Graphic Designing
  • Animation, Multimedia, & Web Designing
