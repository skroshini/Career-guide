Computer Science is the study of computers, programming, and digital systems. It focuses on how technology can solve real-world problems and make life easier. Students learn how software, applications, and networks are built and how they function behind the scenes. This stream is perfect for those who are logical thinkers, enjoy problem-solving, and are interested in how technology works.
